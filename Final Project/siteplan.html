Explore Lambayeque

Site name

Explore Lambayeque

The name "Explore Lambayeque" was selected because it clearly represents a website focused on discovering the attractions, culture, and history of the Lambayeque region. It is simple, descriptive, and easy to remember.

Site Purpose

The website provides information about tourist attractions, museums, archaeological sites, beaches, and cultural destinations in Lambayeque. It helps visitors learn about the region's history, culture, and natural beauty while exploring places to visit.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular tourist attractions in Lambayeque?
  • Which museums and archaeological sites should I visit during my trip?
  • What natural and cultural places can I visit in the region?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color – Warm Brown (#A68A64): Used for headings, navigation, and important elements.
  • Secondary Color – Sand Beige (#D8C3A5): Used for backgrounds and section areas.
  • Accent Color – Soft Green (#8FA67A): Used for buttons, links, and highlights.
  • Text Color – Dark Gray (#3E3E3E): Used for body text and general content.

Typography

  • Heading Font – Playfair Display: Used for headings.
  • Body Font – Roboto: Used for body text, paragraphs and list.
